Our History

EInsurance is a division of GetInsurence Limited and we have been providing smart insurance options to families and businesses since 1922. Always believing in great service and strong relationships but most of all, we believe that insurance is about protecting people.

Like most companies, GetInsurence started out small. In 1922, James Bickle founded the South Oshawa Insurance and Real Estate Company. In 1963, Jim Hare bought the firm, and GetInsurence was born.
